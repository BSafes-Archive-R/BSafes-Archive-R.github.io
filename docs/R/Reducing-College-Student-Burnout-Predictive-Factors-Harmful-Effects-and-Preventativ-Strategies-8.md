## Appendix
### Figure 1

*ERI Model for Educational Contexts*

![ERI Model for Educational Contexts](https://statics.bsafes.com/images/papers/Reducing-College-Student-Burnout-Predictive-Factors-Harmful-Effects-and-Preventativ-Strategies-fig-1.png)

Imbalance maintained…

- If no alternative choice is available

- If accepted for strategic reasons 

- If individual is overcommitted to their work

*Note*. Students who feel that their efforts are not adequately matched by rewards may experience academic burnout, and the contributing factors to such imbalance as mentioned above may be modified through student motivational style and attitude. Adapted from “More Pain than Gain: Effort–Reward Imbalance, Burnout, and Withdrawal Intentions Within a University Student Population,” (Williams et al., 2018).
