## 3. Research Method
The study adopted descriptive survey method. The population of the study involved registered library users of the Rhema University Library, Nigeria, for 2019/2020 academic session which comprises 252 undergraduate students. A total sample size of 100 students was selected using simple random technique. A structured questionnaire was used to get the desired information from the students. The administration of the questionnaire was carried out by the researcher, and a total of 100 copies of the questionnaire were distributed to facilitate responses from the respondents on the spot by the researcher, and 90 copies were completely filled and used for the study. Data collected through the questionnaire were analyzed using frequency counts and percentage.
